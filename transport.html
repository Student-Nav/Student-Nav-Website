Transport

Multiple Tram Routes around Manchester which lead to most surrounding towns.

Nearest major Tram Stop is St. Peters Square, although Picadilly and Picadilly Gardens are similar distances.

Multiple bus routes around Manchester, usually no more than a 5-minute walk to the nearest bus stop.

Three Train stations in Manchester: Oxford Road, Picadilly and Victoria

Trains going through Manchester usually pass through at least 2 stations.

Multiple different car parks located around Manchester.

Mainly NCP or Q-Park.
